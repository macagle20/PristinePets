FAQ

Do you groom large or double-coat breeds?

Yes—subject to coat condition and temperament. Extra time may apply for heavy de-shed or matting.

Do you crate pets?

No. We provide calm, one-on-one care start to finish.

What about anxious or senior pets?

We use low-stress handling and extra time as needed. Share any health notes when booking.

How do I pay?

Card, tap-to-pay, or invoice—your choice. Gratuity goes directly to your groomer.
